Pick up


Clean the planet one piece of trash at a time

Our mission

Pick up is a website designed to entice people to pick up litter they see while going about their daily lives. If everyone forms a habit of doing so, our planet would be a lot cleaner and healthier, not to mention prettier! With every pound of trash picked up, users can start earning points towards gift cards! The more points they earn, the more valueable the gift card is.
