Our mission

Everyday, more and more trash ends up on the streets, in our parks, on our beaches and in our oceans. Everyday we pollute our planet more and more. While we may not be able to strike every day, or pass laws that will help fight against global warming, we are all able to at least clean up a little bit everyday. Pick Up was created in order to help clean up our planet one piece of trash at a time. Together, we can make a change.

How it works

Pick up is a website designed to entice people to pick up litter they see while going about their daily lives. If everyone forms a habit of doing so, our planet would be a lot cleaner and healthier, not to mention prettier! With every pound of trash picked up, users can start earning points towards gift cards! The more points they earn, the more valueable the gift card is.
